   aggregate functions(affregate pipelines)
   ----------------------------------------

    -> aggregate functions are used to perform operations on stage by stage 

    sytax: db.collectionName.aggregate({
     (stage 1),
     (stage 2),
     ..
    }) 

    aggregate operators:
    1. $match
    2. $group
    3. $min
    4. $max
    5. $avg
    6. $count
    7. $lookup( to perform join in MongoDB)
